Help Harry to reach Hogwarts by solving the upcoming challanges!

Choose a card and turn it over by clicking on it. Then selects another card and turn it over.
If the two cards are a matching pair then they stayed turned over and you chose another card. The process continues until all cards on the board will be turned over.
After winning the first game, you have to play two more rounds of memory game. Each winnings will move Harry further in his goal to reach Hogwarts. Getting to Hogwarts would mean the beginning of another long-waited year at Hogwarts which he calls home.
Let the adventure begin!
